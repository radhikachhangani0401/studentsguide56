Additional Campus Facilities

Our college boasts a range of facilities spread across various floors:

  • 1st Floor: Includes Physics, Biology, and Chemistry Labs, a small seminar hall, student washrooms, and a girls' common room.
  • 2nd Floor: Contains classrooms, Teachers' Staff Room (201), Electronics Lab, and Science Department Staff Room.
  • 3rd Floor: Features classrooms, NSS/NCC Room, and Computer Science and IT Labs.
  • 4th Floor: Dedicated to Commerce Section classrooms and student washrooms.
  • 5th Floor: Houses the Library and Awards Room.
  • 6th Floor: Comprises practice rooms, additional classrooms, and our prominent Seminar Hall.

Tip for New Joiners:

For convenience, use the washrooms on the 1st and 4th floors and fill up drinking water from these locations as they are more accessible.
